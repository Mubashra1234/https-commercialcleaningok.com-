About Our Janitorial Services in Oklahoma City – Office Cleaning Specialists

Commercial Cleaning Services of Oklahoma is a leading commercial cleaning company serving the OKC metro area for over 20 years. Based in Oklahoma City, Commercial Cleaning Services of Oklahoma provides a variety of high quality services to customers throughout the area. Regardless of size or type of business, we are confident a solution can be found for your company.

Approach to Cleaning

Commercial Cleaning Services of Oklahoma is committed to providing a good service at a fair price to all customers. We attempt to keep what we do simple. Facilities get dirty and you want us to help you keep them clean. We understand different facilities have different cleaning needs and we pride ourselves on being flexible and qualified to meet those needs. Our hands on – customer minded mentality to business allows for quick responses to emergency situations, and any concerns you may have.

With a focus on customer service, we set our sights on providing everything below and more:

  • On-site inspections
  • Customer communication
  • Rapid response to customer calls 24/7
  • Bonded and insured.
  • Ongoing training for employees including disinfection practices, blood-borne pathogen, etc
  • Safety Data Sheets (SDS) compliance
