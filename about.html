About Us

At Clean Freaks 405, we’re dedicated to making your space shine while keeping your health and the environment in mind. Serving Oklahoma City and surrounding areas, we specialize in 100% non-toxic, eco-friendly cleaning solutions that are safe for your family, pets, employees, and clients. As a locally owned and operated, women-owned family business, we take pride in serving our community with a personal touch. Whether it’s your home or business, we deliver thorough, reliable cleaning services tailored to your specific needs. Our commitment to quality, transparency, and customer satisfaction sets us apart, and we’re proud to be a trusted partner in maintaining clean, healthy spaces across our community. Contact us today to learn more about our solutions to all of your residential and commercial cleaning service needs!

What sets Clean Freaks 405 apart?

  • Free, customized estimates tailored to your company’s specific needs
  • Locally Owned and Operated
  • 100% Non-Toxic Products that are A-Rated by the EWG
  • EPA certified disinfectants that are safe and effective
  • Rapid Response and 24/7 Customer Communication
  • Licensed and Insured
