Commercial Cleaning

Clean Freaks 405 is your trusted partner for commercial cleaning services in OKC and surrounding areas. We understand the importance of maintaining a clean and professional environment for your business, and we’re here to help you do just that with 100% non-toxic, eco-friendly cleaning solutions. From offices to gyms, retail spaces, and medical facilities, our mission is to keep your workspace spotless, safe, and welcoming for employees and clients alike.

We’re committed to providing reliable, high-quality cleaning services that are tailored to your business’s unique needs. Our team of professionals takes pride in delivering exceptional results with the highest level of customer service.

Why Choose Us for Commercial Cleaning?

Eco-Friendly Cleaning Solutions

We prioritize your business’s health and environmental impact. That’s why we use EPA-certified, non-toxic cleaning products that are both effective and safe for employees and clients. Our cleaning methods are tough on dirt but gentle on your workspace and the environment.

Professional and Experienced Team

Our team is fully trained, insured, and dedicated to providing the highest standard of service. We understand the importance of maintaining a professional image, and we ensure your space looks its best every time.

Tailored Pricing for Commercial Spaces

We provide customized quotes based on your facility’s specific cleaning needs. Contact us for a free walkthrough, and we’ll offer a competitive, transparent estimate that suits your budget.

Flexible Scheduling

We know businesses have different operating hours, and we work around your schedule. Whether you need after-hours cleaning or weekend services, we ensure minimal disruption to your operations.

Locally Owned and Operated

As a locally-owned business, we’re deeply invested in the community we serve. We aim to build lasting relationships with our commercial clients by delivering reliable, top-quality service every time.
